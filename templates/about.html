About

So you wanna learn more, eh?

Hi there! Thank you for being here, and welcome to Little Free Kindness! I started this project back in August 2019 with a simple idea: how can I make kindness as accessible as possible? After all, given the pervasiveness of social media and the increasing connectedness of the world, is there some way I can apply that idea for the betterment of our world?

After many failed prototypes, I finally settled on a modeled based off that of Little Free Library. In case you're not aware of what that is, it represents a vast networks of tens of thousands of tiny, community-built libraries. They each can hold a couple dozen books, and the idea is that if you see a book you like, you take it and replace what you took with a book of your choosing. In other words, this entire system is based off of reciprocity and trust.

Indeed, Little Free Kindness works in a similiar fashion. Much like how you're encouraged to replace the book you took, if you do "take" a kind note here, I sincerely hope you "replace" it by submitting a kind note of your own. Not only are you keeping this project alive, you're also having a positive influence on who-knows-how-many people see your note. Anyways, enough of me talking: head back to the main page and start writing!

If you have any questions or comments, feel free to contact me at roger.ji.32021@gmail.com
