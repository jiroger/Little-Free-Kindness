So you wanna learn more, eh?

Hi there! Thank you for being here, and welcome to Little Free Kindness! I started this project back in August 2019 with a simple idea: how can I make kindness as accessible as possible? After all, given the pervasiveness of social media and the increasing connectedness of the world, is there some After many failed prototypes, I finally settled on a modeled based off that of Little Free Library. In case you're not aware of what that is, it represents a vast networks of tens of thousands of tiny, community-built libraries.
